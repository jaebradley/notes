# [8 Most Powerful Life Frameworks That Can Change Your Life in 21 Days](https://medium.com/personal-growth/8-most-powerful-life-frameworks-that-can-change-your-life-in-21-days-d21bcc63e2c8)
* Negative capability: the ability to tolerate ambiguity without rushing to conclusions
  * Sit with a question / ambiguity long enough until it reveals itself properly
  * Saying "I don't know yet" and being ok
  * Important life topics are ambiguous - you will never have complete knowledge
  * Act thoughtfully despite these uncertainties and make peace with the uncertainty
  * "Trying to understand is like straining through muddy water. Have the patience to wait. Be still and allow the mud to settle."
* A lot of advice comes from people that don't have any exposure to your downside
  * Am I taking advice about risk from someone who has never been at risk?
  * Experience (some) exposure to the topics you are interested in
* Chsterton's fence: if you come across a fence in the middle of a road, don't tear it down until you understand why it was built
  * Before you break something that's working imperfectly, understand why it is working at all
* When a problem is hard to solve forwards, try and solve it backwards
* Lindy effect: if something has survived for a long time, it will probably survive for longer
  * This survival is proportional to its current age
