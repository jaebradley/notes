# Chapter 2: What Is a Second Brain?

* The average US employee spends 76 hours per year looking for misplaced notes, items, files
* 26% of a typical knowledge worker's day is pent looking for and consolidating information spread across a variety of systems
  * Only 56% of the time are they able to find the information required to do their jobs
* Think of a note as a "knowledge building block"
  * Discrete unit of information interpreted through your unique perspective and *stored outside your head*
